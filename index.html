Why Fiesta Kitchen

More than a cafe — a community kitchen in the heart of Glen Eden

01

🍽 Homemade Food

From cabinet baking to signature brunch dishes, everything is made fresh in our kitchen daily. No shortcuts, no compromises — ever.

02

☕ Fair Trade Coffee

Every cup brewed with certified fair trade, organic Icoco beans. Great coffee that supports farmers and tastes extraordinary — because both matter.

03

🐶 Dog Friendly

Bring your furry best friend! Our outdoor area welcomes well-behaved dogs. Fresh water bowls always on hand — because great brunches include the whole family.

04

🌿 Vegan Options

Plenty of plant-based options clearly marked on the menu. We offer oat, almond, soy & coconut milk at no extra cost. Flavour-first, always.

⭐ Signature Dish

Fiesta Rice

Our most loved dish — and for good reason. Seasoned Mexican-spiced rice served with a perfectly fried egg, slow-cooked black beans, fresh pico de gallo, cool sour cream, and pickled jalapeños. A bowl of warmth and flavour that keeps people coming back.
