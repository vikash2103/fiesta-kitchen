Our Story

Glen Eden's neighbourhood cafe — homemade food, fair trade organic coffee & Mexican-inspired warmth since 2014

Where It All Began

Fiesta Kitchen opened in Glen Eden in 2014, sister cafe to the beloved Fiesta Coffee Lounge in Pt Chevalier. We serve homemade food, fair trade organic Icoco coffee, with a warm Mexican-inspired atmosphere. Dog friendly, vegan options available, community focused.

From day one, our goal has been simple: cook honest food, brew exceptional coffee, and create a space where everyone feels at home. Step inside for terracotta tones, bold flavours, and the smell of freshly ground beans — Glen Eden has given us so much, and we try to give back every single day.
